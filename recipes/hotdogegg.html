Scrambled Eggs With Hot Dogs

This is a personal recipe of mine, which is made whenever I am hungry and want a quick savory meal to eat. I usually pair this dish up with indomie noodles as they make the perfect combo with the egg helping to dilute the strong flavors of the noodle while the hot dog adds its own delicious texture and taste. I prefer using cheap hot dogs as better and more quality dogs are fit for other dishes while cheaper hot dogs are great as an accompany to other dishes. This recipe only uses 3 simple ingredients and the steps are very few too.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 hot dog wiener
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, crack and pour the two eggs into the bowl and whisk thoroughly, adding a pinch of salt to the egg in order to enhance its flavor
  2. In a small saucepan, add oil to the pan and preheat to medium heat.
  3. While the pan heats up, cut up the hot dog wiener diagonally in order to maximize the surface area of each dog bits, and set aside
  4. When the pan gets hot, add the hot dog and flip over after a minute
  5. After another minute has passed, pour in the eggs and slowly scramble the eggs together.
  6. Place onto a plate and enjoy as-is or with another dish!
